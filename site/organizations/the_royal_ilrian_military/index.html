Ilris

Ilris is a nation in Valmere , spread out across the Valmere midlands. The capital of Ilris is Achenar . It consists of 17 major cities, bordered by the Spine of Kharna to the West, the Stormfall Peaks to the North/North-East, and the Plys Sea to the East.
